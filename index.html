Our Story

Two brothers.
One mission.

All In Care was founded by two brothers — students at the University of South Florida and brothers of Omega Psi Phi Fraternity, Inc. — who saw firsthand how much strain compliance, staffing, and day-to-day operations put on the people running Florida's elder-care communities.

Their mission is simple to state and hard to execute: uplift elder-care business across the state of Florida, building profitable, well-run facilities that never lose sight of the people at the center of them — the residents.

We built All In Care to be the single call an administrator makes when the AHCA binder is overdue, the floor is short-staffed, or the board wants a valuation before a sale. One relationship. Every operational need covered, with the pinnacle of elderly care as the standard we hold ourselves to.

What We Do

One partner. Every operational need.

All In Care replaces a shelf of disconnected vendors with a single accountable team, organized around the four areas that determine whether a facility thrives.

01 · Compliance & Documentation

The paperwork that keeps your license, and your residents, protected.

AHCA Documentation Support

Survey-ready, always. We build and maintain the policies, care plans, incident logs, and compliance files Florida's AHCA surveyors expect to see — so an inspection is a formality, not a fire drill, and your license stays protected.

Documentation & Rubric Dashboard

Retire the binders and scattered spreadsheets. Your compliance documentation and care rubrics live in one live dashboard, giving administrators and owners a real-time, always-current view of facility health at a glance.

02 · Growth & Valuation

Building the reputation and the numbers that make your facility worth more.

Full-Service Marketing & Design

Families choose a facility on the strength of a first impression. We design the brand identity, website, brochures, and campaigns that reflect the warmth and professionalism inside your building — and help fill beds because of it.

Building Valuation & Selling Services

Whether you're planning your next chapter or evaluating an acquisition, we provide accurate, defensible facility valuations and guided selling support — so you capture the true market value of what you've built.

03 · Workforce & Operations

The people, oversight, and daily systems behind consistent resident care.

Workforce Connecting & Hiring Assistance

Staffing shortages are the single biggest threat to consistent care. We connect you with vetted CNAs, nurses, and administrative staff, streamlining hiring so your team spends its time on residents, not endless recruiting.

Building Auditing & General HR Aid

We audit your physical plant and HR practices before a regulator or a lawsuit does — from safety compliance to personnel files — helping you build an operation that's efficient, compliant, and protected.

Dietary Menus & Dietary Budget

Great dietary care is both a regulatory requirement and a quality-of-life cornerstone. We design AHCA-compliant, resident-approved menus while helping you control food costs and reduce waste — protecting wellbeing and your bottom line together.

04 · Technology & Infrastructure

Modern, secure systems built for how care facilities actually operate.

Azure Cloud Infrastructure

We design and implement secure Microsoft Azure environments purpose-built for nursing homes and assisted living facilities — role-based login credentialing, facility-wide connectivity, and modern cloud infrastructure without compromising security.

Stablecoin Secure Payment Infrastructure

We implement secure, blockchain-based stablecoin payment infrastructure — a faster, more transparent, and more secure alternative to legacy processing, reducing transaction costs and settlement delays for your facility.

New Equipment & Workflow Management

From mobility aids to monitoring systems, we manage the full lifecycle of new equipment adoption — sourcing, installation, and staff workflow integration — so new tools improve care instead of disrupting it.
